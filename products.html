What We Offer

Mindfulness Hut

The Mindfulness Hut provides a retreat from the demands and pressures of the regular workday. Unlike a crowded and energy-charged break room, our spaces are designed to evoke feelings of peace and tranquility. As workplaces begin to diversify, these rooms serve many needs and purposes, such as meditation, praying, self-reflection, decompressing stress, or sleep.
