About Us

Alberta Mind Biotech Corporation is a Calgary-based mindfulness biotech company, providing suitable and effective mindfulness courses to achieve efficient results through mindfulness-based interventions, by assisting clients and patients to build up a healthy state of mental, physical and social-well being. Our company provides customized and detail-designed courses to fit diversified mindfulness interests of both individuals and social group clients, by reducing and preventing depression, substance abuse, eating disorders, interactional conflicts, anxiety disorders, and obsessive-compulsive disorder to help clients achieve positive changes in health, attitudes and behaviors. We are committed to help improve social well-being, reduce stress of the unemployed, provide assistance to new immigrates integrating into new environment, find efficient solutions to adolescent issues, improving business efficiency/interaction and facilitating healthy family life. The courses and services on this site are supported by certified professionals of the Chinese Psychological Society Mindfulness Academic Group and Chinese Association for Mental Health Mindfulness Academic Group.
